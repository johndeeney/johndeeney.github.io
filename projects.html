Smoking Cessation App

Smoking Cessation is an Android app that helps people quit smoking using Cognitive Behavioural Therapy and "just in time" prevention. User downloads the app when they are still smoking regularly. They notify the app whenever they smoke, and the app saves the time and location of this smoke break.

When the user indicates that they are starting quitting, all of their smoke breaks are analyzed by a web service using some fancy schmancy machine learning to determine when and where they are most likely to smoke. When they are in that place at that time, the app will send a notification with some sort of positive reinforcement to stay clean.

That app is written in Java (naturally). Database used is SQLite, with ActiveAndroid as the ORM technology. Web service has yet to be designed or implemented, so that's still up in the air.

My Little Bartender

This is a webapp that is designed to help people find cocktails based on the ingredients that they have and/or feel like using. It takes in a list of and outputs a list of recipes that have those ingredients along with additional parameters (e.g. must have a certain ingredient, or can/cannot include cocktails with ingredients not included in the search query).

Backend uses Python's Flask framework. The frontend uses jQuery and Bootstrap. This is currently still being developed, and My Little Bartender is only a working title, as I can't think of any better names that are free. If you have any clever puns to use, please let me know. Unfortunately, Cocktail Snob and Mix Master are taken.
